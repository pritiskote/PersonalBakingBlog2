Banana Bread

Dry Ingredients:

1 3/4 Cup All Purpose Flour

3/4 Cup Cane Sugar

1/2 Tsp Baking Soda

1 Tsp Baking Powder

1/2 Tsp Nutmeg

1 Tsp Cinnamon Powder

1/4 Tsp Cardamom Powder

1 Pinch Salt


Wet Ingredients:

3 Mashed Bananas

2 Eggs

1/2 Vegetable Oil

1 Tsp Vanilla Extract


Instructions:

Pre-heat oven to 350° F.

Mix all dry ingredients.

Mix all wet ingredients.

Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.

Oil the baking pans.

Bake for 30 minutes if you are making a small loaf or 60 minutes for a large loaf. Check on it every 15 minutes.
